Outside of Work

When I'm not building applications, you'll find me pursuing my other passions

Crocheting

Crocheting is my therapy! I love creating small friends and cozy accessories.

Nature

I love anything in nature, from a simple picnic to camping in the mountains. Give me fresh air, green trees, and a good book, and I'm the happiest person alive!

Pet Care

I'm also a pet sitter! I love spending time with pets and taking care of them. If you need someone to spoil your furry friends while you're away, I'm your person!

Reading

Exploring new worlds through books and expanding knowledge. Whether it's romance books, fantasy novels, or biographies, I'm always carrying a book (or my Kindle) everywhere I go.

Hiking

Connecting with nature and staying active outdoors. I love that Germany has so many beautiful forests to explore. Hiking through different forests, each with its own unique atmosphere, is my way of connecting with nature and finding peace.

Baking

I like to bake when I'm bored. Carrot cake, pudim, brigadeiro and other Brazilian desserts. I like experimenting, but like keeping the tradition at the same time. I usually bake sweet stuff for my boyfriend, even though he's diabetic (I swear I'm not trying to kill him).

DIY

I love creating small and meaningful things that I usually gift to people I love. From handmade cards to small crafts, there's something satisfying about creating something unique and personal for the people who matter most.

Podcast

I'm always listening to my favourite podcasts! I usually listen to humor and pop culture podcasts, or podcasts to learn German (because apparently I think listening to German while doing dishes will magically make me fluent).
